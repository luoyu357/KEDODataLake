KEDO Data Lake Testbed

KEDO Data Lake is exploring a KEDO Object that is capable of representing data efficiently in the Data Lake. The research includes 1) developing a smart object service that delivers to a set of logical objects in the DOA space with PIDs and Knowledge Graph, while managing the raw data in the Data Lake, and 2) studying and identifying the benefits of this layered architecture particularly in its potential to build trust at the local level.
